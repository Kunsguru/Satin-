Who We Are:

Livewell Housing Solution Ltd is a premier housing service provider dedicated to easing the financial burden of rent and homeownership.

Our Mission:

To empower individuals and families to secure their desired living spaces, ease the burden of rent with financial flexibility and security.

Our Vision:

To revolutionize the housing market by providing innovative, accessible, and reliable housing solutions.

Core Service Plans

1. Livewell Ease Rents:

Objective: Provide clients with their desired apartment and facilitate ease of rent payment.

Membership Fee: #10,000 (Naira) (non-refundable, one-time payment).

Security Deposit: 50% of the total rent.

Funding Process:

  • Processed within 30-90 days.
  • Payment made directly to the landlord.

Repayment Terms:

  • Payback within 6 to 10 months with a 1.5% monthly interest charge.
  • Default charges applied against the security deposit with incremental interest rates.

2. Livewell Lease Plan:

Objective: Offer long-term rent stability and eliminate rent worries.

Plan Options:

  • 7-Year Rent Plan: Deposit Five year's rent upfront.
  • 15-Year Rent Plan: Deposit ten years' rent upfront.

Loan Facility: Eligible for loans up to 50% of the enrolled package with a 1.5% monthly interest.

Benefits: Extended rent stability and financial flexibility.

3. Livewell Co-operative Community:

Objective: Promote a saving culture and provide financial benefits.

Saving Plan: Save monthly and qualify for loans twice the saved amount after 6 consecutive months.

Membership Fee: #10,000 (Naira) (one time).

Savings: #10,000 (Naira) (monthly payment) minimum and maximum any amount.

Loan Terms: One-time interest fee of 1.5%.

Member Benefits: Free housing benefits and incentives.

Organizational Structure:

Hybrid Operations System.

Key Roles

1. Relationship Officers:

Role: Introduce and explain company plans to potential customers.

Incentives: Regular bonuses and incentives for performance.

Reporting: Directly to Community Managers.

Flexibility: Can operate from anywhere in the country.

2. Community Managers:

Role: Recruit and motivate Relationship Officers.

Innovations: Organize workshops, seminars, and set up communication channels like WhatsApp groups.

Rewards: Recommend officers for rewards and oversee team performance.

Reporting: To Executives.

Team Identity: Create unique team names and earn 10% of team efforts.

3. Executives:

Composition: Manager, Risk Assessment Officers, Secretary, HR, and P.R.O.

Role: Ensure smooth operations, provide solutions, and support the entire organization.

Responsibility: Oversee company operations and maintain operational efficiency.
